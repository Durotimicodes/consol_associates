Company's Approach

CONSOL ASSOCIATES is regarded as one of the top quantity surveying and project management firms in Nigeria and across Africa, we are all about helping property developers save money on construction development and building with less headaches.

Our focus is on “Project delivery at the best costs, best time and best quality value”. At CONSOL, we understand that success of Construction Projects is not achieved in isolation, but through teamwork with Project team members in conjunction with the deployment of our highly trained ethical, cost & procurement specialists.

We align our business to market sectors bringing together like-minded professionals with specialist knowledge and expertise. Our sector specific intelligence enables us to benchmark project criteria against tested deliverables and share innovation and market trends from project to project and sector to sector. We associate with firms in Ghana, South Africa and the UK to achieve international benchmark and deliver customers’ projects to the highest standard.

We are Experienced

Consol has been involved in a couple of successful mergers with some Q.S firms (such as Aderonke Oyelami Associates and Group "Q" Associates) in order to boost the Management Structure and to adequately address our diversification strategy.
We are driven and committed to being an active and exemplary corporate citizen. Consol imbibes their social responsibility into the framework of the company.

A fully Integrated Construction Cost Management Team

The growth strategy of the firm has been hinged on the never ending improvement of service delivery, proper motivation and training of our human capital, adoption of best practices and the provision of an enabling environment that would engender creativity. This we believe would create value and satisfaction that is hard to get elsewhere for our clients.

Though located in Nigeria, we continually strive to ensure that our services compete favourably with that obtainable in the international market. With offices strategically located in Lagos, Abuja and Kaduna, we are positioned to take our services yet further while spreading the experience of the Consol advantage.

CONSOL ASSOCIATES is a corporate member of the London based Commonwealth Association of Surveyors and Land Economist (CASLE), and the Nigerian Institute of Quantity Surveyors (N.I.Q.S).
