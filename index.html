What We Do

Our focus is on “Project delivery at the best costs, best time and best quality value”. At CONSOL, we understand that success of Construction Projects is not achieved in isolation, but through teamwork with Project team members in conjunction with the deployment of our highly trained ethical, cost & procurement specialists.
We carry out building and engineering construction cost management; organize and co-ordinate all consultancy services required in developing any building and engineering projects; design and build light commercial and small residential buildings, and package and administer public-private partnership for concession.
